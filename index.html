How Booking & Payment Works?

Suhani Beauty Parlour me online booking karna bilkul surakshit aur aasan hai:

  • 1. Apni pasand ki service ke neeche "Book Now" par click karein.
  • 2. Aap direct hamare WhatsApp par connect ho jayenge.
  • 3. Hum aapko WhatsApp par PhonePe/GooglePay number ya UPI QR code share karenge.
